Stacked Salads' World!

Salads can be creative and balanced, healthy and tasty, a simple and fulfilling meal, easy to prepare, fresh and rich with nutrients.

I love salads and it hurts me when they're treated just as a sad and clean side dish. I try to give salads the significance they earn, as a complete and succulent course, and I hope my salads can inspire others to try them and enjoy a healthy diet, as I do.
